Our Mission

A Farm Where Everyone Belongs

We believe every person deserves the dignity of meaningful work and genuine connection. Sheep & Giggles partners with the NDIS to provide supported employment, skill-building, and social opportunities in a welcoming farm environment.

  • ✓ Supported employment pathways for NDIS participants
  • ✓ Hands-on farm skills — animal care, horticulture & more
  • ✓ Social connection and community belonging
  • ✓ A safe, inclusive space to build confidence & independence

What We Do

Building Futures on the Farm

Every dollar donated helps us expand our programs and welcome more participants into meaningful, supported roles.

🐑

Supported Employment

Real jobs, real responsibility. Participants work alongside our team caring for animals, maintaining paddocks, and running daily farm operations.

🌱

Skill Development

From animal husbandry to teamwork and time management — participants gain practical, transferable skills in a supportive setting.

🤗

Community & Wellbeing

More than a workplace. Sheep & Giggles is a community where participants build friendships, confidence, and a sense of purpose.
