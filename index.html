Community Farm Project

Sheeps and Giggles Community Farm

A place where people, nature and practical skills grow together.

Sheeps and Giggles Community Farm is a not-for-profit community project where people come together to build a working farm, learn practical life skills and care for the land. Through gardening, permaculture, animal care and environmental restoration we create opportunities for people to connect with nature and develop confidence, skills and community.

Our Mission

A welcoming farm where connection and practical learning meet.

Our mission is to create a welcoming community farm where people can reconnect with nature, learn practical skills and participate in meaningful work on the land.

We believe that working with soil, plants and animals can help people build confidence, independence and a deeper connection to the natural world.

  • ✓ Inclusive community participation through hands-on farm work
  • ✓ Gardening, permaculture and animal care in a real working environment
  • ✓ Practical skill building that supports confidence and independence
  • ✓ Caring for land, habitat and local ecosystems together

What We Do

Practical programs shaped by land, animals and community.

The farm combines real projects, practical learning and community connection so people can contribute in meaningful ways while building useful skills.

🌱

Horticulture & Permaculture

Learning to grow food, build edible gardens and care for the land through seasonal, hands-on growing projects.

🐑

Farm Animal Care

Working with chickens, ducks, sheep and other animals while learning responsible, everyday animal care.

🌿

Environmental Restoration

Revegetation, habitat creation and caring for local ecosystems so the farm supports wildlife as well as people.

🛠

Practical Life Skills

Fencing, landscaping, garden building and real farm projects that build confidence and employment skills.

Community Programs

Participation that supports belonging, learning and contribution.

The farm provides opportunities for people to participate in hands-on projects while learning valuable life and employment skills.

Participants may assist with gardening, building garden beds, fencing, landscaping, animal care and restoring the land.

Programs are designed to welcome volunteers, community members and people seeking disability-inclusive opportunities to learn, contribute and connect in a practical outdoor setting.

Community participation can include:

  • Volunteer days and shared working bees
  • Disability-inclusive farm activities
  • Skill building through practical projects
  • Partnerships with local groups and organisations

Our Vision

A thriving community farm where people, animals and nature flourish together.

We are building for the long term, with space for learning, habitat, food growing and meaningful participation to expand over time.

Our vision is to develop Sheeps and Giggles into a thriving community farm where people, animals and nature can flourish together.

Over time we aim to expand the farm with gardens, wildlife habitat areas and a small animal sanctuary while continuing to create opportunities for learning, connection and community participation.
